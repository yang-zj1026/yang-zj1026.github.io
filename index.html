Hi! I'm currently a visiting student as UCSD advised by Prof. Xiaolong Wang. My research interests lie in Reinforcement Learning and Robotics. I'm interested in applying learning methods for optimal robot policies that can be deployed in the real world.
I earned my MSCS degree from USC, where I worked with Prof. Erdem Bıyık and Prof. Gaurav Sukhatme. Prior to USC, I received my BEng degree in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
