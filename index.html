Hi! I'm Zhaojing. My research interests lie in Reinforcement Learning and Robotics. I'm interested in applying learning methods for general robot policies that can be deployed in the real world.
I earned my MSCS degree from USC, where I worked with Prof. Erdem Bıyık and Prof. Gaurav Sukhatme. Prior to USC, I received my BEng degree in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
